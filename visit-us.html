    The entrance is free for all.

    If you would like a guided tour for your group of 6 or more, please contact us to arrange the tour.

    accessibility

    The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps.

    It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

    food, drinks and shop

    There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

    Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity paks that allows you to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
